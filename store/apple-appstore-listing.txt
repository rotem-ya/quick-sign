Description (English):
QuickSign turns signing a document into a few-second task — all locally on your
device. Receive a document via share, "Open with", or by picking a file (PDF /
JPG / PNG). Tap where to sign, sign by hand or with a saved stamp, and send it
back. The signed document is flattened permanently.

• Full privacy — documents never leave your device.
• Sign by hand or stamp; sign every page at once.
• Extract a stamp from a photo, background removed automatically.
• Text notes in Hebrew and English.
• Rotate pages, drag, resize, delete with undo.
• Export via share / save to any folder.
• Optional backup of signatures & settings to an account (Google / Apple / email).

No ads, no tracking.
